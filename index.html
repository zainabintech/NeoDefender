// Briefing 01

The War for the Matrix

The machines are flooding the system with corrupted code. Pure data and malicious viruses now fall together through the Matrix.

As the Defender, you must tell them apart — and act before the system collapses.

// Briefing 02 — Targets

Know Your Targets

CATCH

Blue Pill — Data

Pure truth keeping the Matrix alive. Click every blue pill before it falls off-screen. Each one saved = points. Let one escape and your system takes the hit.

AVOID

Red Pill — Virus

Corrupted code sent by the machines. Never touch it. Click a red pill and you lose a shield. Just let it fall harmlessly past you.

// Briefing 03 — Defenses

Your Defenses

  • Shields ×5 — five stand between you and collapse. Lose them all and the Matrix falls.
  • Shield Pill — a rare glowing pill. Catch it to restore a lost shield.
  • The Clock — survive 60 seconds to win.
  • Levels — every few seconds the system escalates: more pills, greater speed.
// Mission Briefing

Mission Briefing

  • Click blue pills to score — don't let them escape.
  • Avoid red pills — let them fall.
  • Catch glowing shield pills to recover.
  • Chain catches to build a combo multiplier.
  • Survive the full 60 seconds to win.
